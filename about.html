About KSRM College of Engineering

Vision

To evolve as a center of repute for providing quality academic programs amalgamated with creative learning and research excellence to produce graduates with leadership qualities, ethical and human values to serve the nation.

Mission

  • M1: To provide high-quality education with an enriched curriculum blended with impactful teaching-learning practices.
  • M2: To promote research, entrepreneurship, and innovation through industry collaborations.
  • M3: To produce highly competent professional leaders for contributing to the socio-economic development of the region and the nation.

History

The college owes its existence to the keen interest of Late Kandula Obul Reddy to develop technical education in the Rayalaseema region of Andhra Pradesh. With a view to translating his noble ideal of imparting technical education into reality, a Technical Training Institute was started at Vempalli, Kadapa District in 1979 under the aegis of Sri Kandula Obul Reddy charities.

K.S.R.M. College of Engineering was established in 1980 to perpetuate the memory of Late Sri. Kandula Srinivasa Reddy, youngest son of Late Sri Kandula Obul Reddy. Sri Kandula Srinivasa Reddy, a brilliant student of III year Mechanical Engineering at Delhi College of Engineering, New Delhi, met with his untimely death in a scooter accident on 18th October 1979.

The college was formally inaugurated on 14th November 1980 by Sri T. Anjalah, the Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh, and it started functioning from the academic year 1980-81.
