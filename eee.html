Electrical and Electronics Engineering

Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE)

Vision

To be a leader in electrical and electronics engineering education and research.

Mission

To provide quality education and training in electrical and electronics engineering, fostering innovation and research.

Program Educational Objectives

1. To prepare graduates for successful careers in electrical and electronics engineering.

2. To promote lifelong learning and professional development.

3. To encourage ethical practices and social responsibility.

Program Outcomes

Graduates will be able to:

  • Apply knowledge of mathematics, science, and engineering.
  • Design and conduct experiments, analyze and interpret data.
  • Design systems or processes to meet desired needs.
  • Work effectively in teams.
  • Identify and solve engineering problems.
  • Communicate effectively in various formats.
  • Understand the societal impact of engineering solutions.
  • Engage in lifelong learning.
  • Stay updated with contemporary issues.
  • Utilize modern engineering tools.

Courses Offered

  • Circuit Theory
  • Control Systems
  • Power Systems
  • Digital Electronics
  • Microprocessors
  • Signal Processing
