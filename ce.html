Civil Engineering (CE)

Vision

To be a leader in innovative and sustainable civil engineering solutions for the betterment of society.

Mission

To provide quality education and training in civil engineering, promoting research and development in the field.

Program Educational Objectives

1. To prepare graduates to excel in their professional careers in civil engineering.

2. To encourage graduates to engage in lifelong learning and professional development.

3. To instill a sense of ethical responsibility and commitment to sustainable practices.

Program Outcomes

Graduates will be able to:

  • Apply knowledge of mathematics, science, and engineering.
  • Design and conduct experiments, as well as analyze and interpret data.
  • Design a system, component, or process to meet desired needs.
  • Function on multidisciplinary teams.
  • Identify, formulate, and solve engineering problems.
  • Understand professional and ethical responsibility.
  • Communicate effectively.
  • Understand the impact of engineering solutions in a global, economic, environmental, and societal context.
  • Engage in lifelong learning.
  • Stay informed of contemporary issues.
  • Use the techniques, skills, and modern engineering tools necessary for engineering practice.

Courses Offered

  • Structural Analysis
  • Geotechnical Engineering
  • Transportation Engineering
  • Water Resources Engineering
  • Environmental Engineering
  • Construction Management
