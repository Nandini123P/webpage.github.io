Humanities and Sciences (HS)

Vision

To provide a strong foundation in humanities and sciences, fostering critical thinking and ethical responsibility.

Mission

To enhance the overall educational experience of engineering students through quality education in humanities and sciences.

Program Educational Objectives

1. To prepare students for effective communication and critical thinking.

2. To instill ethical values and social responsibility.

3. To promote interdisciplinary learning and research.

Program Outcomes

Graduates will be able to:

  • Communicate effectively in both oral and written forms.
  • Demonstrate critical thinking and problem-solving skills.
  • Understand the ethical implications of their work.
  • Work effectively in teams and collaborate with others.
  • Engage in lifelong learning and self-improvement.

Courses Offered

  • Engineering Mathematics
  • Physics for Engineers
  • Chemistry for Engineers
  • Technical Communication
  • Environmental Studies
  • Professional Ethics
