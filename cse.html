Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)

Vision

To be a leader in computer science education and research, fostering innovation and excellence.

Mission

To provide quality education and training in computer science and engineering, promoting research and development.

Program Educational Objectives

1. To prepare graduates for successful careers in computer science and engineering.

2. To promote lifelong learning and professional development.

3. To encourage ethical practices and social responsibility.

Program Outcomes

Graduates will be able to:

  • Apply knowledge of computing and mathematics.
  • Analyze problems and identify computing requirements.
  • Design, implement, and evaluate computer-based systems.
  • Function effectively on teams.
  • Understand professional, ethical, legal, security, and social issues.
  • Communicate effectively with a range of audiences.
  • Analyze the impact of computing on individuals, organizations, and society.
  • Engage in lifelong learning.
  • Stay updated with contemporary issues.
  • Use current techniques, skills, and tools necessary for computing practice.

Courses Offered

  • Data Structures
  • Algorithms
  • Database Management Systems
  • Operating Systems
  • Software Engineering
  • Web Technologies
